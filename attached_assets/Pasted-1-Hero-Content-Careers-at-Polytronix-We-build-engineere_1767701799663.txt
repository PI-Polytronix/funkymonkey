3. Role Categories
 
Engineering
 
Support design, problem solving, and build readiness for electronic systems
 
Work with manufacturing and quality to ensure products meet specs
 
Participate in reviews and technical discussions
 
 
Production
 
Build assemblies and products according to process documentation
 
Follow test procedures and record results
 
Work with engineering to solve issues that arise in build and test
 
 
Quality & Compliance
 
Perform inspections, documentation checks, and audits
 
Support corrective actions and process improvements
 
Work with internal teams and external customers on quality requirements
 
 
Sales
 
Work with customers on requirements, pricing, and delivery timelines
 
Coordinate internally with engineering and operations
 
Support proposals and contract discussions
 
 
Corporate (Support)
 
Provide finance, operations support, and administrative functions
 
Help coordinate cross-functional activities
 
Support business continuity and internal systems

5. How We Work (Operating Reality)
 
Engineering provides clear documentation and build intent
 
Manufacturing follows documented procedures and reports deviations
 
Quality is part of the build process, not only an after-the-fact inspection
 
Teams communicate directly to resolve issues
 
Individuals take responsibility for their work outcomes
 
Programs are typically ongoing with repeat builds
