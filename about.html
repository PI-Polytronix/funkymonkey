Mission-Critical Manufacturing Since 1981

Polytronix builds rugged displays, panels, and integrated electronics for aerospace, defense, and industrial systems. Design, machining, assembly, and testing under one roof.

What We Do

We manufacture displays, electro-mechanical assemblies, cable harnesses, and integrated systems for programs where reliability matters. Our capabilities span from prototype through production, with in-house engineering, optical bonding, ruggedization testing, and FAA-certified repair services.
