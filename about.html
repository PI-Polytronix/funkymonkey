Highly Engineered Manufacturing since 1981

History

From concept through test, Polytronix provides design, precision machining, assembly, and validation for mission-critical components across aerospace & Defense, medical, and industrial applications. Since 1981, we have been at the forefront of manufacturing and sub-assembly for highly engineered components and sub-assemblies. When displays and electronic assemblies fail, programs stop. For us, failure is not an option. We build for the most demanding environments where quality, reliability, and technical accuracy matter.

Polytronix, Inc., headquartered in Richardson, Texas, has been an innovator in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of liquid crystal displays for over 40 years. Our displays provide visual output across medical, aviation, aerospace, marine, automotive, industrial controls, and Defense applications. In aerospace and military markets—where quality and reliability are non-negotiable—we are a recognized leader in Dichroic, Heilmeier, and PWTN technology, with IPC-A-610 Certified IPC Trainers and J-STD-001 qualified specialists supporting every electronic assembly. We serve the largest aerospace companies worldwide with custom instrument display panels built for tomorrow’s requirements.
