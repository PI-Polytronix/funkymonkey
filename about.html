Highly Engineered Manufacturing and Sub-Assembly Since 1981

Polytronix builds highly engineered components for the aerospace & defense, medical, and industrial industries. Design, machining, assembly, and testing under one roof.

What We Do

Since 1981, Polytronix has been at the forefront of manufacturing and sub-assembly for highly engineered components and sub-assemblies. When displays and electronic assemblies fail, programs stop. For us, failure is not an option. We build for the most demanding envrionments where quality, reliability, and technical accuracy matter.

Example Applications

  • Aviation cockpit displays and panels
  • Satellite & space sub-assemblies
  • Patient monitoring and surgical equipment
  • Industrial robotics and factory equipment
  • HVAC controllers and interfaces
