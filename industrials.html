Industrial and commercial electronics, displays, and panels

Equipment builders in transportation, energy, medical, and general industrial markets need rugged human machine interfaces and electronics that stay in service in real world duty cycles. Polytronix helps teams move from prototype to production with displays, panels, and integrated assemblies that fit the application and the business case.

  • Shorten the path from concept to production ready HMI and electronics
  • Reduce field failures with assemblies designed for harsh and dirty environments
  • Support low to mid volume, high mix production without losing control of cost
