Overview

In-house optical bonding eliminates the air gap between display layers. Process supports AR coatings, NVIS filters, EMI shielding, and touch sensor integration into unified display assemblies.

Technical Specifications

DeliverablesOptically bonded display assemblies, cover glass integration
Integration TypesWet bonding, dry bonding (PSA), AR/AG coatings, NVIS filters
Ruggedization OptionsImpact resistance, thermal cycling stability, moisture sealing
Environmental Properties-40°C to +85°C operating, improved contrast ratio, reduced reflection
