Certifications and compliance

Polytronix quality systems and compliance programs support aerospace, defense, and industrial customers that work in regulated and high consequence environments.

This page gives an overview of our core certifications and how they support your internal quality and regulatory needs.

Quality system certifications

Summary of our primary quality system certifications.

Standard Scope How it helps you
AS9100D Quality management system for aerospace and defense Supports aerospace and defense program requirements, audits, and customer approvals.
ISO 9001:2015 General quality management system Provides a documented, repeatable process framework across design, production, and support.

Current certificates and detailed scope statements are available on request for your supplier approval process.

Industry and regulatory alignment

We work with customers that must meet a mix of aerospace, defense, and environmental expectations.

Area Typical reference Notes
Aerospace environmental test DO-160 style test plans We design and build displays and assemblies that can support DO-160 style environmental testing when required by the customer.
Defense environmental and EMI MIL-STD style test plans We support customer led test programs and provide data about materials and construction as part of the process.
Environmental compliance RoHS and REACH We can provide RoHS and REACH related statements and work with your team on specific material questions.
